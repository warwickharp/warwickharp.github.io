Warwick Harp About Contact Diary

Caitlin Hughes is one of the leading harp students in the UK, and will shortly start taking bookings for professional appearances in 2019. Despite her age, Caitlin is a calm and accomplished musician, happy to work with you to find the perfect music for your event.

Based in the county town of Warwick, she is available for events anywhere in Warwickshire, or the surrounding area.

  • Caitlin studies harp & violin at the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire, and regularly performs with their Junior Symphony Orchestra.
  • Caitlin performs with the National Children's Orchestra - she will be part of the “Under 13” orchestra during 2018.
  • Caitlin leads the Warwickshire Central area orchestra on violin and harp.
  • Caitlin attends Kings High School in Warwick, where leads and plays in a wide range of groups & orchestras.

If you are interested in contacting Caitlin to discuss opportunities, please email contact@warwickharp.co.uk, and we will get back to you as soon as we can.
