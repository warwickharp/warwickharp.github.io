Caitlin Hughes is one of the leading harp students in the UK; despite her age, Caitlin is a calm and accomplished musician, happy to work with you to find and perform the perfect music for your event.

Based in the county town of Warwick, she is available for events anywhere in Warwickshire, or the surrounding area.

  • Caitlin studies harp as part of the Junior Academy at the Royal Academy of Music.
  • Caitlin studied harp & violin at the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire from 2015 to 2018, and regularly performed with their Junior Symphony Orchestra.
  • Caitlin is a music scholar at King's High School in Warwick, where she leads and plays in a wide range of groups & orchestras.
  • Caitlin performs, on the harp, with the National Children's Orchestra - she will be part of the “Under 13” orchestra during 2018.

If you are interested in contacting Caitlin to discuss opportunities, please email contact@warwickharp.co.uk, and we will get back to you as soon as we can.
